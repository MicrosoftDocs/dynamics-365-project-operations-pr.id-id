## <a name="set-up-bookable-resource-as-a-pricing-dimension"></a><span data-ttu-id="547c1-168">Konfigurasi sumber daya yang dapat dipesan sebagai dimensi harga</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="547c1-168">Set up bookable resource as a pricing dimension</span></span>
